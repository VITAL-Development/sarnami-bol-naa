# unit-13-reading — candidate example sentences

Status: DRAFT

Source: `authored_docs/byakaran/11-texts.md` (Sarnami Byākaran, Marhé 1985, Bijlagen 1–3: two
folk tales plus a fable). Per issue #261, this draft uses **extraction, not generation** — every
candidate below is copied verbatim (Sarnami + existing Dutch gloss) straight from the text,
rather than derived from a grammar rule and invented. There is no independent second
"grammatical derivation" verify pass; the `verify:` line instead confirms accurate transcription
of both the Sarnami and the Dutch, and that the sentence is reasonably self-contained. Rows I
could not confidently source a genuine, attested Dutch translation for (see lesson 3 note) were
left out rather than paraphrased or invented.
